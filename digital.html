Introduction to Digital Marketing

Digital Marketing involves using various digital channels (like search engines, social media, and email) to promote products or services. This course is designed to equip you with the essential strategies and tools needed to drive online business growth, build brand awareness, and engage a target audience effectively.

Key Skills You Will Master

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) : Driving organic website traffic.
  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC) : Managing paid ad campaigns on Google Ads and Meta.
  • Social Media Marketing (SMM) : Strategy and execution across major platforms.
  • Content Marketing : Creating high-quality, engaging content.
  • Web Analytics : Using tools like Google Analytics to measure performance.
  • Email Marketing & Automation.
