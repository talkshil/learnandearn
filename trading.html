Introduction to Trading

Trading is the buying and selling of financial instruments (like stocks, commodities, or currencies) with the goal of making a profit in a short time frame. This course provides a comprehensive foundation, focusing on developing a disciplined approach to analyzing market movements and managing the inherent risks.

Key Trading Skills You Will Master

  • Technical Analysis : Reading charts, patterns, and indicators (e.g., RSI, Moving Averages).
  • Fundamental Analysis : Evaluating a company's financial health.
  • Risk & Money Management : Setting stop-loss, position sizing, and capital preservation.
  • Trading Psychology : Controlling emotions and maintaining discipline.
  • Understanding Derivatives (Futures & Options).
  • Using Trading Platforms & Brokerage Tools.

Detailed Course Modules

  1. Introduction to Financial Markets & Terminology
  2. Candlestick Patterns and Charting Basics
  3. Advanced Technical Indicators & Strategy Development
  4. Risk Management and Position Sizing
  5. Introduction to Derivatives (F&O)
  6. Live Trading Sessions and Practical Application
  7. Trading Psychology and Journaling

Certification and Practical Application

The course concludes with a focus on practical readiness:

  • Simulation Trading : Prove proficiency on a live-market simulator.
  • Strategy Presentation : Develop and defend a robust trading strategy.
  • Final Theory Exam : Test your knowledge of market principles and tools.
